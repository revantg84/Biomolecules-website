What are Nucleic Acids?

Nucleic acids are complex organic molecules that store and transmit genetic information. They are made of nucleotide chains and are essential for the functioning of living organisms.

Types of Nucleic Acids

  • DNA (Deoxyribonucleic Acid)
  • RNA (Ribonucleic Acid)

Functions

  • Store genetic information
  • Transfer genetic information during reproduction
  • Direct protein synthesis (via RNA)

Examples

  • DNA found in chromosomes
  • RNA in ribosomes
  • mRNA, tRNA, rRNA
